From The Earth, To You...

We do our best to hunt and choose the highest quality material available in Washington State. Located in small-town Darrington, we take pride in and enjoy "the hunt" for high quality material, and love to share our creations with the world! Visit the Gallery page for stories and experiences we have had during our excursions, and if you are curious, a brief inside look into the crafting process. If you are interested in buying our products, or simply wish to browse what is available, pop into the store and check out the catalog of available products. If we dont have what you are looking for, dont be afraid to inquire for custom work on the contact page!
